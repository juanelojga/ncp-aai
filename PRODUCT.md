# Product

## Register

product

## Users

NCP-AAI certification candidates studying in focused daily sessions, usually on a personal machine with a local source corpus, generated notes, and quiz history. The primary user is technical and wants evidence-backed study material without losing time to source management.

## Product Purpose

NCP-AAI is a local-first study platform that turns exam objectives, trusted source material, retrieval, and investigation jobs into a repeatable study loop. Success means the learner can identify weak objectives, inspect grounded notes and citations, quiz one concept at a time, and start follow-up investigations from gaps without leaving the app.

## Brand Personality

Quiet, precise, and operational. The interface should feel like a trusted study console: serious enough for certification prep, compact enough for daily repetition, and transparent about evidence and system state.

## Anti-references

Avoid marketing landing pages, oversized hero layouts, decorative AI-workflow visuals, purple-blue gradient SaaS styling, chat-only interfaces that hide source evidence, and playful gamified study surfaces. The app should not look like a generic startup dashboard or a course marketplace.

## Design Principles

- Retrieval first: answers, notes, and quiz feedback should foreground sources and provenance.
- Daily workflow density: prioritize scanning, comparison, and repeated action over decorative spaciousness.
- Local system transparency: paths, provider status, jobs, and empty states should make the backend state understandable.
- Progressive depth: dashboard summaries should lead into objective, topic, source, and job detail without modal detours.
- Provisional honesty: readiness and coverage should distinguish real data from placeholders.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.1 AA for contrast, focus states, keyboard navigation, and form labeling. Motion should be minimal and state-based, with reduced-motion support. Do not rely on color alone for correctness, status, or readiness indicators.
